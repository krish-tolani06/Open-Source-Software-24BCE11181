 Open Source Manifesto
----------------------------------------
Date: 31 March 2026

I believe in the power of open source.
Using tools like git, I have experienced how collaboration can improve technology.
For me, freedom means open source, and it is an important part of software development.
In the future, I would like to build wifi intrusion detection system and share it with others without restrictions.
I support the idea of open knowledge and community-driven innovation.
